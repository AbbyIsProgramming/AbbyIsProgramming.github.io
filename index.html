About Me

Hi there! My name is Abby Rochelle. I'm a second year Game Design and Development student at the Rochester institute of Technology. I have a passion for computer programming, narrative design, user interfaces, game design, and interactive media. I designed and programmed this website as a digital Game Design portfolio. Projects

The Flowershop; Business is Blooming!

This original game demo won my team an award at RIT's Women in Computing 2025 Hackathon. As the Game Engineer, I programmed our demo in HTML and JavaScript. Recently, my team has began the process of rebuilding and expanding our game in Unity.

Pizza Is Ready!

Last semester, in IGME 106: 'Game Development and Algorithmic Problem Solving II', I, alongside a team of my peers, utilized the MonoGame Framework to create our final project: 'Pizza is Ready!' I designed the lifecycle of the game, the Drag and Drop functionality, as well as loading and saving game information from a text file.

Bee Flocking Simulation

This semester, I've been honing my skills in C# and Unity. To demonstrate my understanding of automated non-player characters, I developed a 2D Unity project that contains a swarm of bees who's movements are determined by my flocking and seeking algorithm.
